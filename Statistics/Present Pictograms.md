- A pictogram (also known as a pictograph) uses pictures to represent the frequency instead of columns

| Number of messages  | 0   | 1   | 2   | 3   | 4   | 5   | 6   | 7   | 8   |
| ------------------- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Number of teenagers | 2   | 5   | 9   | 7   | 3   | 8   | 6   | 5   | 4   |
- A pictogram is effectively just a bar chart that uses pictures to represent amounts
- You **NEED** a key to tell the reader what one image means
![[statsPictograph.png]]

- A pictogram can represent any value with each image
- Any frequency in between those values can be represented using a proportion of the image
