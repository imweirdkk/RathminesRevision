- A cumulative frequency table shows the frequencies as a running total
- We can turn a grouped frequency table into a cumulative frequency table by adding frequencies as we go and rewriting the groups:


Grouped Frequency Table:

| Time (minutes)     | 0-5 | 5-10 | 10-15 | 15-20 | 20-25 | 25-30 |
| ------------------ | --- | ---- | ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- |
| Number of students | 4   | 8    | 11    | 6     | 0     | 1     |
Cumulative Frequency Table:

| Time (minutes)     | <5  | <10 | <15 | <20 | <25 | <30 |
| ------------------ | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Number of students | 4   | 12  | 23  | 29  | 29  | 30  |
