Deep learning is a subset of machine learning. It uses large neural networks and big data sets to learn on its own without human supervision

# The Key Characteristics of Deep Learning
- It is a multi-layered concept
- It runs on neural networks
- It is machine learning with a hierarchical approach
- It needs a large volume of data for processing

# Consider Some Examples

## Deep learning in Driverless Cars

Here, the deep learning technology continuously renders the car's surrounding environment, like street signals, other vehicles, humans, and animals, making predictions of events that have a relatively high possibility of occurring 

# Generative AI

GenAI uses enormous neural networks, trained using an a huge amount of data, to generate text, audio, images, and movies from text.

Tools like ChatGPT, an AI chatbot, listen's to the user's input, in the form of natural language, and then generates and output in the form of text, pictures, and audio using artificial intelligence.
