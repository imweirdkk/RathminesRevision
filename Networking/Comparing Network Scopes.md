
| Type                                  | Advantages                                                                                                        | Disadvantages                                                                                       | Applications                                                                              |
| ------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[PAN - Personal Area Networks\|PAN]] | - Low cost<br>- highly secure<br>- very easy to establish                                                         | - ~10m range<br>- Generally slow transfer speed                                                     | - Connecting Bluetooth headphones, or personal IoT devices                                |
| [[LAN - Local Area Networks\|LAN]]    | - High Bandwidth<br>- Low Latency<br>- Enables easy sharing of local resources (printers, files)                  | - One building or campus<br>- Initial wiring can be costly                                          | - Corporate office networks<br>- School computer labs<br>- Home WiFi setups<br>           |
| [[CAN - Campus Area Networks\|CAN]]   | - Organisation retains full ownership<br>- High-speed fibre backbone<br>- Covers multi-building sites             | - Quite expensive<br>- Requires complex routing                                                     | - University Campuses<br>- Large corporate headquarters<br>- Military bases               |
| [[SAN - Storage Area Networks\|SAN]]  | - Extremely fast<br>- Block-level data access<br>- Scalable<br>- Offloads heavy storage traffic from the main LAN | - Very expensive specialised hardware (Fibre Channel)<br>- Very complex to configure and manage     | - Enterprise data centres<br>- High volume database storage<br>- Virtual Machine clusters |
| [[WAN - Wide Area Networks\|WAN]]     | - Massive geographical reach<br>- Seamlessly connects disparate local networks across the globe                   | - High ongoing service/leasing costs<br>- Significantly higher latency<br>- Increased security risk | - The Internet<br>- Multinational corporate links<br>- ISP backbone infrastructure        |
