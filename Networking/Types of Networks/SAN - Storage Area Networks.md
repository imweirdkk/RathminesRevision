**Definition:**
- A specialised, high-speed network providing block-level network access to storage arrays
**Purpose:**
- Uncouples storage from specific servers, allowing multiple servers to share a consolidated storage pool.
**Technologies:**
- Fibre Channel (FC)
- iSCSI
- Dedicated SAN switches
- Disk array

**Challenges:**
- High cost of implementation
- Complex management
- Requires highly specialised IT skills
