**Definition:** 
- A PAN connects devices within the immediate vicinity of a single person (typically up to 10 meters)

**Purpose:**
- Enables personal devices to sync, share data, or connect to a larger network without cables

**Technologies:**
- Bluetooth
- USB
- Zigbee
- Near Field Communication (NFC)

**Challenge:**
- Extremely limited range
- Potential interference from other wireless technologies
