**Definition:**
- A LAN connects computers and devices withing a limited geographical area, such as a home, school, or office building
**Purpose:**
- Facilitates high-speed sharing of local resources (files, printers, internal applications)
**Technologies:**
- Ethernet (copper/fibre)
- Wi-Fi
- Switches
- Wireless Access Points (WAPs)

**Challenges:**
- Managing broadcast traffic
- Physical cabling constraints
- Local network security
