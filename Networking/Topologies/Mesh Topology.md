**Positives**:
- Very reliable; many paths available
- Great for WANs and backbones

**Negatives**:
- More links means higher costs and complexity
- Needs more routing/switching control

**Use cases**:
- Partial or full-mesh WANs (MPLS, SD-WAN)
- Wireless mesh for wide coverage

**Extra notes**:
- *Full mesh*: every site connects to every other. 
- *Partial mesh*: only key sites are fully interconnected
