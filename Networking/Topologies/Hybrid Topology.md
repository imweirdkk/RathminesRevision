**Topologies use in the picture**:
- There is a [[Ring Topology|ring (single)]] topology that leads into a [[Bus Topology|bus]] topology going into a [[Star Topology|star]] topology

**Key ideas**:
- Real networks mix topologies: 
- I.E: [[Star Topology|star]] at access, [[Tree (Hierarchical) Topology|tree]] in distribution, and a [[Mesh Topology|mesh]] for a
	resilient core. Choose what best fits the
	building, budget, and performance goals.

**Why use hybrid**:
- Balances cost and uptime
- Keeps failures local and easily scalable 
- Uses the best topology on each layer
