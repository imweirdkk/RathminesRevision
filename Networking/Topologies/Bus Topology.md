**Positives**:
- Very simple and cheap to cable
- Fast to set up for small, short-term labs

**Negatives**:
- One backbone faults brings the network down
- Collisions are common; length and device count is limited

**Use cases**:
- Older Ethernet (10BASE2/10BASE5); temporary or ad-hoc setups.

**Extra notes**:
- The ends are terminated making trouble shooting more difficult
